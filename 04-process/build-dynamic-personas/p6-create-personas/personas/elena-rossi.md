Instead of disengaging, they built compensating habits: clearer prompts, explicit constraints, and faster review loops. These habits made them more effective and more selective at the same time. In daily life, this persona is best understood as a disciplined adapter: open to improvement, grounded in evidence, and focused on meaningful outcomes. Their journey is less about becoming dependent on AI and more about designing a reliable human-plus-AI workflow that preserves agency while improving performance.
